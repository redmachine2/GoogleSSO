Angular Google Plus Sign-in Directive

Usage

  1. Include SSO.js.
  2. Add GoogleSSO as a dependency to your app and inject SSO into login controller.
  3. Add <google-sign-in clientid="your-client-id">
  4. SSO.loggedIn is a promise that resolves when authenitcated and rejects when authentication fails
